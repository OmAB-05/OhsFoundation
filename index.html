Mission Statement

Olympia High School Foundation's sole purpose is to preserve and enhance the quality of education at OHS by raising money to meet specific OHS academic needs.

Since our start in 2009 we have donated more than $15,000 to Olympia High School for items such as microscopes, textbooks, choir robes, and student tutoring.

100% of your tax deductible contribution will be used this year to fund teacher identified classroom needs at Olympia High School.
